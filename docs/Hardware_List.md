# Sports Bar TV Controller - Hardware List

## Overview

This document provides a comprehensive hardware list with Amazon purchase links for deploying the Sports Bar TV Controller system across three different tiers: Basic, Standard, and Enterprise. Each tier is designed to meet different operational requirements and budgets.

## Deployment Tiers

### Basic Tier (2-8 TVs)
**Target:** Small sports bars, cafes, or establishments with limited TV displays
**Budget Range:** $3,000 - $5,000

### Standard Tier (8-16 TVs)
**Target:** Medium-sized sports bars with multiple viewing areas
**Budget Range:** $8,000 - $12,000

### Enterprise Tier (16+ TVs)
**Target:** Large sports bars, entertainment complexes, or multi-location deployments
**Budget Range:** $15,000 - $25,000
