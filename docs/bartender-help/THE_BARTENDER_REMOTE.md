# The Bartender Remote — Start Here

*This is the "learn the whole thing in 10 minutes" guide. If you've never touched the
iPad behind the bar before, read this first. For step-by-step help with one specific
job (putting a game on a TV, fixing a wrong channel, etc.) there's a focused guide for
each — they're linked at the bottom.*

---

## What it is

The **bartender remote** is the app on the iPad (or tablet) behind the bar. It's your
one place to:

- Put any game or channel on any TV
- Change channels on the cable boxes, satellite (DirecTV) boxes, and Fire TV sticks
- Turn TVs on and off
- Set the volume in each part of the bar
- Play and change the background music
- Find what game is on what channel tonight

You **cannot break anything** by tapping around. Nothing here charges money, deletes
data, or calls anyone. The worst that happens is a TV shows the wrong thing for a few
seconds and you tap again. So explore freely.

> The remote only controls TVs, audio, and music. It does **not** control the cash
> register, the kitchen, or anything else.

---

## How to open it

It's usually already open on the bar iPad. If the screen is dark, tap it to wake it.
If you closed it by accident, tap the **Sports Bar** icon on the iPad home screen, or
open the browser to the address taped near the iPad (it looks like `http://…:3002`).

If it asks for a **PIN**, that's the staff PIN your manager gave you.

---

## The tabs along the bottom

The buttons across the bottom are how you move around. Here's what each one is for —
you'll mostly live in the first three.

| Tab | What it's for | Use it when… |
|-----|---------------|--------------|
| **Video** | A picture/map of the bar. Tap a TV, pick what to show. | This is the easy way to change a TV. **Start here.** |
| **Guide** | Tonight's games and what channel they're on. | A customer asks "is the game on?" Tap **Watch** and it goes up. |
| **Schedule** | Games coming up later, and setting them to come on automatically. | Getting ready for the day's games before the rush. |
| **Routing** | A full grid of every source × every TV. | The Video tab isn't enough, or you want the "power user" view. |
| **Remote** | An on-screen remote for one cable box / DirecTV / Fire TV. | You need to punch in a channel number or press buttons like a normal remote. |
| **Audio** | Volume for each part of the bar. | A section is too loud or too quiet. |
| **Music** | The background music (play, pause, skip, change the playlist). | Changing the vibe / someone wants the music down. |
| **Power** | Turn TVs on and off; switch a TV's input. | Opening, closing, or a TV is on the wrong input. |

You may also see **Lighting** or **DJ Mode** if your bar has those — most don't.

## The five things you'll actually do

### 1) Put something on a specific TV  *(the most common job)*

**Easiest way — Video tab:**
1. Tap **Video**.
2. If your bar has rooms (Main Bar, Patio…), tap the room with the TV you want.
3. **Tap the TV** on the map.
4. A list of sources pops up (cable boxes, DirecTV, Fire TVs, etc.). **Tap the one you want.**
5. You'll see a green ✓ confirmation. The TV changes in a couple seconds.

If nothing changes after ~10 seconds, just tap the source again. → Full guide:
[Putting games on TVs](PUTTING_GAMES_ON_TVS_VIDEO_TAB.md)

### 2) Find a game and put it up

1. Tap **Guide**.
2. Type a team or league in the search box (e.g. "Packers"), or scroll the list.
3. Find the game — it shows the time and what channel it's on.
4. Tap the **Watch** button next to it. It goes to the main TV (or launches the app on a
   Fire TV if it's a streaming game).

→ Full guide: [Finding a live game](FINDING_A_LIVE_GAME.md)

### 3) Change the channel on a cable / satellite box

1. Tap **Remote**.
2. On the left, **tap the box** you want (e.g. "Cable Box 1").
3. An on-screen remote appears on the right.
4. **Tap the channel number** on the keypad (like "5", "0", "6"). It tunes after a
   moment, or tap **Enter**.
5. Faster: tap one of the **preset buttons** (ESPN, Red Zone, etc.) under the keypad.

→ Full guides: [Wrong channel on a TV](WRONG_CHANNEL_ON_TV.md) ·
[Channel presets](change-channel-preset.md)

### 4) Change the volume in a part of the bar

1. Tap **Audio**.
2. Find the area you want (Main Bar, Patio…).
3. **Drag its slider** up (louder) or down (quieter), or tap **Mute**.

The volume here is for the **bar's speakers**, not the TV's own little speakers.
→ Full guide: [Audio zones and groups](AUDIO_ZONES_AND_GROUPS.md)

### 5) Change the music

1. Tap **Music**.
2. **Play/Pause** is the big button. **Skip** jumps to the next song.
3. Tap a **playlist/station** tile to change the vibe (it switches and starts playing).

→ Full guide: [Music or audio problem](MUSIC_OR_AUDIO_PROBLEM.md)

## Reading the screen

- **Green ✓ pop-up** = it worked. These disappear on their own after a second or two —
  you don't need to dismiss them.
- **Red ✗ pop-up** = it didn't work. Just try the same tap again. If it keeps failing,
  see the troubleshooting links below.
- **"…ing" with a spinner** (Routing…, Tuning…) = it's working on it; give it a moment.
- **Green dot** next to a TV or device = it's on and reachable. **Gray/red dot** = it's
  off or unplugged.
- **A channel name under a TV** (e.g. "ESPN") = what that TV is showing right now.

## When something looks wrong

Work down this list — most issues are one of the first two:

1. **Tap it again.** Commands occasionally don't land the first time. A second tap fixes
   most things.
2. **Give it 10 seconds.** The TV and boxes are real hardware; they're not instant.
3. **Wrong channel on a TV?** → [Wrong channel on a TV](WRONG_CHANNEL_ON_TV.md)
4. **A TV is black / off / on the wrong input?** → [Power & network TVs](POWER_AND_NETWORK_TVS.md)
5. **The remote itself won't load or respond?** → [Remote isn't working](remote-isnt-working.md)
6. **Audio or music problem?** → [Music or audio problem](MUSIC_OR_AUDIO_PROBLEM.md)

You will **not** make anything worse by trying. If you're truly stuck, take a photo of
the screen and text your manager — that's exactly the right move.
