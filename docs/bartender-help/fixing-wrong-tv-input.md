# A TV Is Showing the Wrong Thing — Which Fix Do I Need?

A customer says a TV is "wrong." That can mean three different things, and each
has a different quick fix. This page tells you which one you've got in 10 seconds,
then sends you to the right guide. **You can't break anything by trying.**

## 10-second sort

Look at the TV and pick the line that matches:

| What you see | What it actually is | Go to |
|---|---|---|
| Right game/show but **wrong channel**, or you want a **different game** | Right source, wrong tuning | [Wrong channel on a TV](WRONG_CHANNEL_ON_TV.md) |
| **Black / "No Signal" / static**, or it's showing **a totally different feed** than the other TVs | Pointed at the wrong **source** | Use the **Video tab** (below) |
| TV is **off** or a network TV won't power on | Power, not source | [Power & network TVs](POWER_AND_NETWORK_TVS.md) |

Most "wrong" complaints are the **first row** (channel) — start there if you're
not sure.

## The middle case: a TV is on the wrong SOURCE

"Source" = where the picture comes from: a Cable Box, a DirecTV box, or a Fire TV.
Every TV can be pointed at any source. "Wrong source" means it's pointed at the
wrong box — so it shows another TV's feed, or goes black because that box is off.

1. On the iPad, open the **Video** tab.
2. **Tap the TV** that's wrong (use the room tabs up top to find it).
3. Tap the **source** you want for it (the correct Cable Box / DirecTV / Fire TV).
4. Give it ~5 seconds to switch.
5. Need a specific game now? Send it from the **Guide** tab → tap the game → tap
   the TV. (Full steps: [Wrong channel on a TV](WRONG_CHANNEL_ON_TV.md).)

If switching the source fixed the black screen but it's now the wrong *game*,
that's the channel fix — see the first row above.

## The rare case: a network TV stuck on its own wrong HDMI input

A few TVs are "network TVs" (the Video tab can't always recover them if someone
used a **stray TV remote** to change the TV's own input). On those, the network-TV
tile shows little **HDMI Input** buttons labeled **1 · 2 · 3 · 4**.

- You'll almost never touch these — the Video tab handles sources for you.
- But if one specific TV won't show the right picture no matter what you pick on
  the Video tab, tap **1, 2, 3, 4** one at a time until the right source appears.
- Full detail: [Power & network TVs](POWER_AND_NETWORK_TVS.md).

## When to tell a manager

- A whole **room** of TVs goes wrong at once.
- The same source box is **black on every TV** every time you pick it.
- A single TV stays black no matter which source **or** HDMI input you choose.

Switching sources and channels is exactly what the remote is built to do — none of
it can hurt the system, so try the steps above freely before escalating.
