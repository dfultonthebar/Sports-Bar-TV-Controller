# App Won't Launch on Fire TV

**Plain English for behind the bar.**

If a streaming app (ESPN, Prime, etc.) won't open on a Fire TV:

1. On the iPad Remote tab, tap the TV tile.
2. Look for the app grid or presets.
3. Tap the app again — wait 10 seconds.
4. If still stuck: tap the power icon on that tile to restart the Fire TV (takes ~45 seconds).
5. Try the app one more time.

Can't break it — the system logs every try. If nothing works after restart, text the manager with the TV number.
