# How to Schedule a Game (let the system tune your TVs for you)

**Who this is for:** any bartender or manager who wants tonight's games to show up
on the right TVs **automatically**, without standing there changing every channel
by hand at game time.

---

## What "scheduling" actually does

You tell the system **which game** you want on **which TVs**, and when the game
starts it **routes those TVs and tunes the channel for you** — automatically. No
running around with the remote at kickoff. When the game ends, those TVs go back
to their normal default channels on their own.

Think of it like setting a DVR, but for your whole wall of TVs.

---

## The easy way — let the AI pick (AI Suggest)

This is the fastest way, and most nights it's all you need.

1. Open the bartender remote and tap the **Schedule** tab.
2. Tap **AI Suggest**.
3. Wait a few seconds — it reads tonight's games and suggests **which game goes on
   which TVs**, putting the home teams (Packers, Brewers, Bucks, Badgers) front and
   center.
4. Look over the list. Tap **Approve** on the ones you want. (You can skip any you
   don't.)
5. Done. Those games will tune themselves on at game time.

That's it. You don't have to know channel numbers — the system figures them out.

---

## The manual way — pick it yourself

Want a specific game on specific TVs? You can set it by hand:

1. **Schedule** tab → find the game in the list.
2. Pick the **TVs** you want it on (tap them).
3. Tap **Save / Schedule**.

The system tunes those TVs to that game when it starts.

---

## Good to know

- **You're never locked out.** If you change a channel by hand after a game is
  scheduled, that wins — the system notices and remembers your choice.
- **Every morning at 4 AM**, all the TVs reset to their normal default channels, so
  you always start the day clean. (Anything muted stays muted — a bartender unmutes
  at open.)
- **A game already playing** that's confirmed live won't get yanked off a TV — the
  system protects in-progress games.
- **Nothing happens to a TV you didn't schedule.** Un-scheduled TVs just keep doing
  their normal thing.

---

## When to use it

- **Busy game nights** — set the marquee games once in the afternoon, then just
  pour drinks. The TVs handle themselves.
- **Home-team games** — schedule the Packers/Brewers/Bucks/Badgers game so it's
  guaranteed on the big TVs at kickoff.
- **Overlapping games** — let AI Suggest spread them across the room so every game
  has a TV.

If something looks wrong, you can always grab the remote and change any TV by hand —
scheduling never takes that away from you.
