# Turning TVs On or Off for Open or Close

When opening or closing the bar, you may need to power the TVs on or off.

## To turn TVs on at open:
1. On the bartender iPad, open the Sports Bar app.
2. Go to the Power section.
3. Tap "Turn All TVs On".
4. Wait 30 seconds for TVs to warm up.

## To turn TVs off at close:
1. On the bartender iPad, tap "Turn All TVs Off".
2. Confirm the action.

If some TVs don't respond, check the power strips or call the manager. The system uses IR blasters for power commands since CEC is disabled on cable boxes.

This is the standard open/close routine.
