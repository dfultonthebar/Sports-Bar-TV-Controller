## Channel Entry Guide

### Entering Channel Numbers

**Cable Box Method:**
```
1. Press numbers on remote
2. Press ENTER or OK
3. Wait 1-2 seconds

Example: ESPN (206)
  Press: 2 → 0 → 6 → ENTER
```

**System Preset Method:**
```
1. Go to Guide tab
2. Select cable box
3. Tap preset button
4. Instant tune

Example: ESPN
  Guide → Device → [ESPN Button]
```

### Sub-Channels

Some channels have sub-channels (e.g., 5.1, 5.2):
```
Press: 5 → . (or DASH) → 1 → ENTER
```

## Troubleshooting

### "Channel Not Available"
- **Check package:** May not be subscribed
- **Check listings:** May be wrong channel number
- **Check blackout:** Game may be blacked out locally

### "No Signal"
- **Check cable box:** Power on? Green light?
- **Check connections:** HDMI cables secure?
- **Check channel:** Try different channel

### Channel Won't Change
- **Check device status:** Online in system?
- **Check IR emitter:** Positioned correctly? (IR devices)
- **Try again:** May have timed out
- **Use physical remote:** Backup method
